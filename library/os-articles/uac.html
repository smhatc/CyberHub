Privilege management involves managing and assisting users privileges to reduce security risks,and ensure that it follows organizational policies. Hoe It works? User Account control (UAC) It works by prompting users when a process requires permission to access it. This is preventing from unauthorized application from making wide changes. It organizes applications and actions into different privileges levels and requires administrator authentication for important changes. Privilege escalation prevention Privileges management helps reduce privilege escalation attacks by ensuring that users and applications have only the minimum necessary permissions. It uses the principle of least privilege (PoLP), it reduces the risk of malware and other threats. Role- Based Access control (RBAC) RBAC is responsible to assign permissions based on user roles, and ensuring that only authorized personnel can access to sensitive system resources. It will reduce potential risks by limiting unnecessary access. Just in time privileges JIT is a security practice that give privilege granted to access applications or systems in a limited period of time on a specific tasks to do. This helps to reduce security vulnerabilities, and prevents to give permanent administrative privileges to unauthorized users. Benefits of UAC and Privilege management 1.
